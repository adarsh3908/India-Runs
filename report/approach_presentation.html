  • Weighted average of platform signals

Mitigating Chronological Honeypots

Why We Isolated 274 Profiles

The hackathon brief estimated ~80 honeypots. Our chronological validator captured exactly 274 unique candidates with zero false positives. Every single flagged profile has physical anomalies:

  • Startup Launch Conflicts (250 caught): Claiming work at CRED before 2018, Sarvam AI before 2023, or Krutrim before 2023.
  • Job Duration Mismatch (16 caught): Experience duration exceeds date ranges (e.g. 171 months at Stark Industries in a 21-month calendar window).
  • Empty Skill Durations (8 caught): Listing expert proficiency with 0 months of experience.

Track 01: Data & AI Challenge

Beyond Keyword Matching: Semantic Alignment

1. Dynamic Token Similarity

Instead of matching exact strings against a closed list, we tokenize and compute overlap. This scores related terms naturally.

  • Token overlap Jaccard similarity
  • Character 3-gram fallback for fuzzy matches
  • Credits synonyms like ColBERT, hybrid search, and dense passage retrieval without hardcoding

2. Title Relevance & Experience Curves

Ensuring candidates have target experience levels while filtering out non-technical applicants.

  • Experience Fit Curve: Peaks at 5.0 to 9.0 years YoE. Severe penalty for junior profiles (< 3 yrs YoE).
  • Disqualified Titles Filter: Instant disqualification penalty (-50 pts) for Accountant, Marketing Manager, civil engineers, etc.
  • Description keyword matching: Scores history description relevance against JD keywords.

Career & Engagement

Track 01: Data & AI Challenge

Career Modifiers & Behavioral Signals

Continuous Consulting Ratio Penalty

The job description penalizes careers spent entirely in consulting/services giants (TCS, Infosys, Wipro, Accenture, Genpact, Deloitte, PwC, etc.).

  • Avoids binary all-or-nothing gates
  • Calculates ratio: sum(consulting_months) / sum(total_months)
  • Multiplier: 1.0 - 0.5 * consulting_ratio (scales smoothly from 0.5 to 1.0)

Weighted Platform Signal Modifiers

To avoid compound multipliers which skew scores, we calculate a weighted average of platform signals:

  • Recruiter Response Rate (weight 3)
  • Notice Period (weight 2) - bonus for sub-30 days, penalty for >90 days
  • Inactivity date decay (weight 2)
  • Open to Work flag (weight 1)
  • Score Cap: Clipped multiplier to [0.5, 1.2] to prevent extreme swings
